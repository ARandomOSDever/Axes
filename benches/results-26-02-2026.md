Benchmarks (Taffy 0.9.2 from crates.io vs Axes)

Runned on a Ryzen 5 5625U

| Benchmark     | Axes      | Taffy     | Difference |
|:-----------:|:-------:|:-------:|:--------:|
| 1,000 Nodes   | 10.579 µs | 79.892 µs | 153.227%    |
| 10,000 Nodes  | 717.93 µs | 993.74 µs | 32.227%    |
| 100,000 Nodes | 8.2757 ms | 32.340 ms | 118.498%    |

Axes is about 2x faster than Taffy, but Taffy has way more features.
